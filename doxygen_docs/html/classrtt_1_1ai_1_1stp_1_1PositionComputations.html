Determines the location for defenders around the defense area Uses the defence area boundaries and the path from ball to center of goal to find the intersects of circles to find the various positions.

Parameters
field
world
amountDefendersto be placed
Returns
vector with Vector2 positions for each of the defenders

Get defense area border geometry

Find intersect of ball to goal on the border of the defense area

Intersect found with defense area: Place robots around the intersect in front of defense area

No intersects with defense area: The ball should be outside the field if this happens. Place robots in the same spot everytime when this happens, if no positions it segfaults.
